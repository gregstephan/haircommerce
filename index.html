What Is HairHuddle?

HairHuddle is a great new way for hairdressers to make money without any upfront costs. The way it works is simple. After you sign up we'll make you an online store with a great range of hair. Instead of referring clients to other beauty shops, direct them to your one. You'll then make money from each purchase! HairHuddle ships the order out and even pays you automatically at the end of each month.

HairHuddle is officially launching in July 2015 with a select group of hairdressers. They will received extra benefits such as one-on-one support and privileged access to new features. If you want to be part of this group, submit your name and email address today!
